Our Facilities

At New Horizons, we want you to feel welcome and at home. Our accommodations offer a bright dining hall, spacious walkways, state of the art therapy rooms and lovely outdoor spaces, we always want you and your loved ones to feel comfortable and cared for during your stay with us.

We’re committed to give you a safe and peaceful environment that you can call your second home. We’ll do our best to give you wellness, every day of your stay.
