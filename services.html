Care Services

New Horizons' services are designed to help seniors improve their physical strength, health, and independence following serious medical procedures and orconditions. We offer rehabilitation services for short-term inpatients, home health care, outpatient rehabilitation, and long-term skilled nursing care by highly trained professionals. We also offer an in-home rehabilitation program to make the transition from the rehab center to independent living smooth and safe.

Each patient that enters our rehabilitation program receives a customized treatment plan based on their specific medical condition. We track their progress on regular intervals and make adjustments to make sure that they are always improving to recover in physical strength, and health. We are committed to the highest standards of clinical care and service. Our professional staff includes occupational therapists, physical therapists, and speech-language pathologists focused on your health and recovery.

Other rehabilitation services we have include:

  • Speech therapy
  • Neurological, orthopedic, cardiac, and pulmonary rehabilitation
  • Strength and coordination, and strength training
  • Therapies for pain management, incontinence, and muscle atrophy
  • Balance and gait assessment/fall-risk assessment
  • Individual fitness programs
  • Aquatic therapy and water aerobics
  • Health education
  • Senior spas with specialized wellness equipment
  • Adaptive equipment assessment and training
  • Home safety evaluation and modifications

We also make sure that the environment and facilities of the rehabilitation center is safe and peaceful to ensure happy hormones that will fasten the recovery of our patients

Dining

Many healthcare establishments hire outside catering companies, but for us, we are proud to produce all our meals ourselves. Whether you're enjoying our "Chef's Special" or an item off of our "Select Menu," our meals are freshly made. by our own cooking staff

We bake our own pastries, rolls and bread. Our desserts are freshly prepared, and our meals' ingredients are fresh from the market. We offer fresh fruits and vegetables, and extra servings are always available.

Our meal program is nutritionally balanced and approved by dieticians to meet the California State standards of nutrition. At New Horizons, patients have the option of customizing their menus to their personal preference. We feature in-room, outdoor, therapeutic, and social dining experiences for our patients.

Entertainment

Relaxation is an important part of your total recovery. Studies show that maintaining a healthy and positive psycho-social well-being helps promote the healing process.

Here at New Horizons, we offer a variety of patient centered events for you to be a part of. Whether you prefer individual activities, or choose to join in our daily social group activities, our staff is here to help promote your well-being.

Some of your entertainment options include:

  • Personal Cable Television Services with individual Television Sets
  • Complimentary Individual Telephone Services
  • Portable DVD Players with a Movie Library
  • Patient Reading Library with Books on Tape available Recreational Garden Activities
  • Family friendly, Park-like Grounds
  • Wireless Internet Services with available bedside workstations
  • Beautician Services

Some of our Activities include:

  • Movie Nights
  • Family Candle Light Dinners
  • Bingo Nights
  • Musical Events
  • Resident Council
